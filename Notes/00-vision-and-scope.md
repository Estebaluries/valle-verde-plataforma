# Inmobiliaria Valle Verde – Vision & Scope

## Long-Term Vision
Build a digital ecosystem that evolves from a simple real estate website into a full operational platform that manages:
- Property listings
- Agents and commissions
- Clients and leads
- Appointments and follow-ups
- Payments and contracts
- CRM workflows
- Admin dashboards
- Analytics and reporting

## Mission
Provide a modern, efficient, and transparent digital experience for clients, agents, and administrators.

## Phase Roadmap
### Phase 1 – Website (Current)
- Static pages
- Property catalog
- Contact form

### Phase 2 – Dynamic Listings
- Admin uploads properties
- Search, filters, categories
- Agent assignment

### Phase 3 – CRM System
- Lead management
- Follow-up tracking
- Agent performance

### Phase 4 – Client Portal
- Saved properties
- Appointment scheduling
- Document uploads

### Phase 5 – Full Platform
- Payments
- Digital contracts
- Automated workflows
- Analytics dashboards
